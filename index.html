ARBITRAGE SIMPLY EXPLAINED

ARBITRAGE IS TRADING THAT EXPLOITS THE TINY
DIFFERENCES IN PRICE BETWEEN IDENTICAL ASSETS
IN TWO OR MORE MARKETS (EXCHANGES).
THE ARBITRAGE TRADER BUYS THE ASSET IN ONE
MARKET AND SELLS IT IN THE OTHER MARKET
AT THE SAME TIME IN ORDER TO POCKET
THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN THE TWO PRICES
